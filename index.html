Mission Manifest

The goal of the Mars Exploration Program is to explore Mars and to provide a continuous flow of scientific information and discovery through a carefully selected series of robotic orbiters, landers and mobile laboratories interconnected by a high-bandwidth Mars/Earth communication network.

Photos

The photos used in this section were taken from the rovers (Curiosity, Opportunity and Spirit) that are currently on Mars. Some of the pictures that could be seen include shots from the 'Mast Camera', 'Navigation Camera', 'Panoramic Camera', 'Mars Descent Imager' and 'Mars Hand Lens Imager'.
